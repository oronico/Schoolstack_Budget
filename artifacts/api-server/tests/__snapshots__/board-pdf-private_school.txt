Recommended Next Steps
1. Strengthen Tuition Revenue Base
Private schools typically derive 60–85% of revenue from tuition. At 16.2%, your tuition revenue share is
lower than typical, so ensure your pricing reflects the full cost of education and is competitive for your
market.
This strengthens the model and reduces future risk.
2. Revenue Projection Jump from Prior Year
Year 1 projects 64.7% revenue growth over last year's actual $2,400,000. Growth over 30% in a single year
requires clear justification: enrollment surge, new funding stream, or tuition increase.
This strengthens the model and reduces future risk.
3. Address Cash Reserve Deficit from Prior Year
Last year ended with $120,000 in cash, only 0.7 months of expenses. Building reserves to 3+ months
should be a priority. Consider a bridge line of credit while growing into net income.
Addressing this will materially improve financial position.
