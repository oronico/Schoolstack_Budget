Feature 6: Per-Pupil Cost Breakdown on Dashboard
Why: Bellwether shows cost/pupil at the school level (line 72: "School Costs/pupil" and line 73: "Surplus(Deficit)/pupil"). SchoolStack shows revenue per student but not cost per student by function. Lenders and authorizers benchmark instructional spend per pupil, facility cost per pupil, and admin cost per pupil against state and national averages.
Current state:

Financial Health dashboard in addDashboardSheet() has "Revenue per Student" (line ~rpsRow)
No expense-per-student metrics
Expense categories exist and are tagged on every expense row (instructional_program, occupancy_facility, administrative_general, etc.)
computeExpenseForYear could be called per-category by filtering rows
