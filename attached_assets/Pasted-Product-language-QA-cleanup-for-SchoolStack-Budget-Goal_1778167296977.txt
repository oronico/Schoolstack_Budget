Product-language QA cleanup for SchoolStack Budget.

Goal:
Founder-facing language must consistently position SchoolStack Budget as a school-facing planning and learning tool, not a bank underwriting system. Lender-grade outputs are allowed, but the founder experience should not feel like a credit decision or loan approval workflow.

Fix these items:

1. Strengthen founder-voice tests

Find:
founder-voice.test.ts or any founder-facing copy/language test file.

Update the banned or restricted term list to include:
- approved
- declined
- ineligible
- failed
- fail
- passed
- pass/fail
- credit decision
- underwriting decision
- loan approval
- approval packet
- bank determination
- borrower approval
- rejection
- rejected

Allow only narrow exceptions:
- internal test names
- API status strings not shown to founders
- legal/privacy language where unavoidable

- “may raise questions”

“SchoolStack Budget underwrites your school.”

It should not say:
“You pass/fail underwriting.”
