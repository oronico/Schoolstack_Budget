PHASE 4. Workbook validation

For the workbook downloaded from the beginner microschool scenario:

Open in Excel or Google Sheets.

Pass criteria:
- Workbook opens.
- No corruption warning.
- No undefined.
- No NaN.
- No #REF!.
- No #DIV/0!.
- No #VALUE!.
- School name appears correctly.
- Year 1 revenue is credible.
- For 30 students, $12,000 tuition, and 95% collection, Year 1 tuition revenue should be approximately $342,000.
- Year 5 revenue should be credible, not millions.
- Export labels are founder-facing:
  - Founder Planning Workbook
  - 1-Year Operating Budget
  - 5-Year Financial Model
  - Lender Conversation Snapshot
- No founder-facing export says:
  - credit memo
  - underwriting decision
  - loan approval packet
  - approval packet

If revenue is inflated into the millions for the default scenario, mark NO GO.
