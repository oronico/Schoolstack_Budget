URGENT UNDERWRITING FIX SPRINT

Repo:
oronico/Schoolstack_Budget

File to prioritize:
artifacts/school-financial-model/src/pages/underwriting.tsx

Problem:
The public guest wizard is technically live, but it is not underwriting-ready. It also appears to build payloads that may not conform to the canonical model schema.

Critical evidence:
1. Guest wizard buildModelDataPayload creates revenueRows with categories:
   - "tuition"
   - "publicFunding"
   - "philanthropy"

   But the canonical schema expects:
   - "tuition_and_fees"
   - "tuition_offsets"
   - "public_funding"
   - "school_choice"
   - "grants_contributions"
   - "philanthropy"
   - "other_revenue"

2. Guest wizard uses driverType "fixed" in places, but the canonical schema and engines use "annual_fixed", "monthly", "per_student", "percent_of_base", etc.

3. Guest wizard appears to pre-multiply per-student revenue in amounts, then also labels the row driverType as "per_student". That risks double-counting revenue because the canonical engine multiplies per_student amounts by enrollment.

4. Proof pack smoke output shows a 30-student microschool producing $21,069,525 in Year 5 revenue and 99.1% profit margin. That is not credible and suggests payload mapping or formula mismatch.

Do this first. Do not add new features until the math is fixed.
