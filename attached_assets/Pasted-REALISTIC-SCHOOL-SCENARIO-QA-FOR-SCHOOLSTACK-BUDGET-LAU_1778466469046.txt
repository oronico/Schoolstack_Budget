TEST REQUIREMENTS FOR EACH SCENARIO

For each scenario, test:
1. Readiness analysis runs.
2. Excel export downloads.
3. Workbook opens.
4. Workbook contains no:
   - undefined
   - NaN
   - #REF!
   - #DIV/0!
   - #VALUE!
   - #NAME?
5. Revenue is mathematically credible.
6. Staffing ratio is reasonable or properly flagged.
7. Facility burden is reasonable or properly flagged.
8. Founder compensation gap is flagged when missing.
9. Enrollment evidence gap is flagged when weak.
10. Public funding timing risk is flagged when relevant.
11. Debt service is reflected in estimated debt cushion or readiness language when debt exists.
12. Tone is founder-safe and educational.

BANNED FOUNDER-FACING LANGUAGE

Founder-facing readiness copy, export copy, and scenario output should not say:
- approved
- declined
- rejected
- ineligible
- failed
- pass/fail
- credit decision
- underwriting decision
- loan approval
- approval packet
- bank determination

Allowed language:
- worth strengthening
- needs more clarity
- may raise questions
- a lender or funder may ask about this
- your model gets stronger when...
- useful for loan-readiness conversations
- before sharing externally

BUG CLASSIFICATION

P0 Launch Blocker:
- readiness analysis fails
- Excel export fails
- workbook corrupt
- workbook contains broken formulas or NaN/undefined
- revenue math is clearly inflated or impossible
- scary approval/decline/credit-decision language appears
- app crashes or route becomes unusable

P1 Fix Soon:
- expected risk not flagged
- confusing tone
- minor export label inconsistency
- workbook opens but a summary label is unclear
- mobile usability issue that does not block completion

P2 Post-Launch:
- deeper CFO-mode enhancement
- more nuanced grant narrative
- full assumption confidence expansion
- additional charts
- improved board/funder narrative
